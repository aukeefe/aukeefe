Wotif

Wotif is an online travel booking service that has positioned itself as a low cost, last minute flight and hotel provider. Wotif.com has been ranked by Alexa one of the top 15,000 trafficked websites on the internet.

Task

The task is to create an On-Site marketing modal box on Wotif's hotel selection page. The objective is to encourage customers to continue with their hotel booking before they leave or close the web browser. The design needs to include a coupon code which customers can redeem on the payment page.

Idea

After looking through Wotif's brand creatives and guidelines, I found that client have some excellent videos and I want to incorporate that into the design.

For this design, I will embed the video for the desktop version while I'll disable the video tag in mobile devices and replace it with GIF version. This works better as GIF file will load faster on mobile devices.

Design

The challenging part of the design was to create the hover effect of the "Get Coupon" button. I hand-coded the "peel effect" using pure CSS. After some testing, I managed to get the result that I wanted. The design is responsive and works on all desktop and mobile devices.
