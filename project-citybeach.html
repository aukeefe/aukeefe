City Beach

City Beach is an Australian fashion, surf, skate and street chain store. Founded in 1985, City Beach has grown to over 60 store locations across Australia and an online store.

Task

City Beach wants a cart recovery email template to encourage customers to go back to their website and continue shopping. We need to design three different templates - men's, women's and mixed templates.

Project Type

Email Marketing, Email Design

Project Responsibilities

Idea, Research, Design, Build & Testing, HTML & CSS.

Tools used

Adobe Photoshop, Sublime Text, InVision, Litmus

Idea

City Beach has a variety of brands that targets to a young and trendy audience. For this project, I gathered some of their brand images and creatives to form a mood board. I want the main banner in each template to be fun and stylish that speak to their audience.

Mood Board
Typography
On-Site Color Palette

Build & Testing

Email design is hard when it comes to testing. I need to make sure the templates work across major email clients. With Litmus, I can upload the email template and get instant previews in over 50+ email clients.

One of the challenges was to make sure the banner looks good on mobile devices. For mobile devices, I hide the background image using media queries and display a mobile version image.

Litmus testing

Design

The email templates not only works great in all email clients but also show an increased rate in cart recovery sales.
