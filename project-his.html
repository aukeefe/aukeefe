H.I.S.

H.I.S. is a travel agency in Japan, specializing in low-cost package tours. In Japan, H.I.S. has 303 branches throughout the country and a global network of 185 branches in 124 cities abroad.

Task

The assignment is to create an On-Site marketing modal box on H.I.S.'s flight payment page. The design needs to have a form for the users to email a copy of their flight selection to their email address so that they can continue booking at a later stage.

Project Type

On-Site Marketing

Project Responsibilities

Idea, Research, Design, Build & Testing, Animation, HTML & CSS.

Tools used

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dreamweaver, InVision

Idea

I want the modal box to look like a boarding pass ticket that has the customer's flight selection information and also a form for them to fill in their email address so that they can continue booking later.

Design
